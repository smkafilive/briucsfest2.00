About BRIU CS FEST

Brahmaputra International University, Jamalpur

BRIU CS FEST 2.0 is the flagship annual Computer Science Festival organized by the prestigious Brahmaputra International University, Jamalpur. This three-day extravaganza brings together the brightest minds in technology, innovation, and creativity to celebrate the limitless possibilities of computer science.

Our mission is to create an ecosystem that fosters collaboration, knowledge sharing, and groundbreaking innovation. We provide a platform for students, professionals, and tech enthusiasts to showcase their talents, learn from industry experts, and network with like-minded individuals.

This year's theme, "Empowering Innovation and Creativity," focuses on how technology can transform ideas into reality, pushing the boundaries of what's possible in fields like artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, data science, and software engineering.
